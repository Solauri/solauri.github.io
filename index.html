Who Am I?

I'm a guy who enjoys making things; I go by Solauri online. I've dabbled in many creative hobbies prior to computer programming such as: Music, Illustration, 3D Art, Digital Art and Creative Writing. One day in college, I wanted to make a game so I tried programming for the first time and I fell in love immediately. As the years went by, I discovered front-end development and was blown away by the idea of combining computer programming and art together. As I continue my web development journey, I'll continue updating this site with the new things I'm learning.

So far, I've learned about the importance of semantic elements. It's great for SEO and organization in the HTML document. I've also learned a ton about CSS text and color properties. I'm struggling a lot with layouts and some more complex designs, but I'll still keep reading and learning.
